## Savory Institute Enrollment Readiness

To be eligible for EOV enrollment, we need:

- [ ] At least 1 full year of baseline monitoring data
- [ ] All STM sites assessed at least 4 times (once per season)
- [ ] At least 1 round of LTM soil sampling completed
- [ ] At least 1 round of water infiltration testing completed
- [ ] Photo points documented for all seasons at all sites
- [ ] Demonstrable improving or stable EHI trend
- [ ] Monitoring plan documented and followed consistently

**Target enrollment date:** Spring 2028

---

## How Goals Connect to Management

| Goal | Management Action |
|------|------------------|
| Reduce bare soil | Maintain ground cover through grazing management, cover crops |
| Improve infiltration | Build soil structure through root diversity, reduce compaction |
| Increase organic matter | Leave litter, manage grazing for recovery periods |
| Boost biodiversity | Multi-species grazing, pollinator strips, riparian plantings |
| Reduce erosion | Maintain cover, improve water infiltration, protect steep areas |

---

*These goals will be refined after the first year of baseline data. Targets should be reviewed annually and adjusted based on what the monitoring data shows.*
