# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this farm management
project a respectful and productive experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the farm operation
- Showing empathy toward other team members

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**

- Disrespectful or dismissive comments
- Personal attacks or insults
- Sharing others' private information without permission
- Conduct that disrupts productive collaboration

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all interactions within this
repository, including Issues, Pull Requests, and Discussions.

## Enforcement

Team members who violate these standards may be asked to correct
their behavior. Repeated violations may result in restricted access.

## Questions

If you have concerns, reach out to the farm manager directly.
